About me

My name is Germán Jáuregui and I am a Product Designer focused on creating memorable and impactful user experiences. My passion for design has driven me to explore various fields, from UX/UI to web development and 3D modelling.

With a strong foundation in product design, I’ve contributed to projects that are not only visually compelling but also deliver significant value to users. My goal is to turn ideas into functional, engaging realities.

Outside of work, I find balance and emotional stability through outdoor sports and music. I love surfing and playing the guitar, and I’ve had the opportunity to share my music on digital platforms, conveying my essence and energy.
